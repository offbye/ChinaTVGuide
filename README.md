# ChinaTVGuide
The source code of my first Android App on Google market in 2010, it has been top 30 in google market Entertainment catagory,  not maintained in 2012

On Google Play
https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.offbye.chinatvguide


ChinaTVGuide is your best friend when you watching TV.It provide TV program guide in China.It also provide  entertainment news, TV rates，and TV program recommendations.Now it covers about 800 TV channels in China.
It is comparable with Android 1.5-3.1。
